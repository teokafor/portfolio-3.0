Stack Master

Stack Master is an online, single-player card game inspired by other games like Bejeweled, 2048, and Wordle. Stack cards of the right type together and make chains to get massive bonuses! How many points can you get?

Built with React and Vanilla JS.

Play the game here!

SocialSpot

A simple social media platform tied directly into a user's Spotify account. Connect with friends, show off your favorite albums and artists, and see you and your friends' listening statistics.

Built with Firebase and Vanilla JS.

Song Scraper

A CLI-based tool that combines the handcrafted nature of a DJ's playlist with the total control of a streaming service like Spotify. Export song data directly from online radio stations, either to .csv files or straight into a spotify playlist of your choice (requires access to Spotify for developers.)

Built with Python, BeautifulSoup4, and Spotify's API.
